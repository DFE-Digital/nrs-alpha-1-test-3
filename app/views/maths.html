Maths level 2

This course can help you develop the practical skills you need to use maths in life, study and work.

You’ll develop your maths skills and learn how to use them in real-life situations. By reaching Level 2, you’ll reach the equivalent to a C or higher grade at GCSE, meaning that you can demonstrate your ability to understand and use everyday maths at this level. This course also includes everything you need at Level 1 so is perfect if you need something to help you develop maths skills and confidence.

Full time

  • takes around 2 weeks
  • is 35 hours a week study
  • available in classroom, online or both
